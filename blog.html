Recipe

Here is the recipe for the Vegan Cold Brewed Coffee:

  • 1 cup of coarsely ground coffee
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1/4 cup of almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • Sweetener of your choice (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a large jar or pitcher, combine the coarsely ground coffee and cold water.
  2. Stir well and let it steep in the refrigerator for 12-24 hours.
  3. After steeping, strain the coffee using a fine mesh sieve or coffee filter.
  4. Serve over ice, add almond milk, and sweeten to taste.

Enjoy your refreshing Vegan Cold Brewed Coffee!

Tips:

  • Experiment with different coffee beans for unique flavors.
  • Adjust the steeping time for a stronger or milder brew.
  • Add flavored syrups or spices for extra taste.
  • Try different plant-based milks for variety.
  • Serve with a sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder for garnish.

We hope you enjoy making and sipping on this delicious Vegan Cold Brewed Coffee. It's a perfect drink for any time of the day!
